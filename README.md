## üëã Welcome <a name="introduction"></a>
**VigyBag Winter of Code** is an organization that helps students and professionals contribute to the open-source community, enhancing their skills while adding valuable contributions to the world of open source. This is a 45-day contribution program aimed at empowering individuals to make a difference.

<h3><em>Join us in bridging the gap between your skills and the open-source world!</em></h3>

## üìå Repository <a name="repo"></a>

Tired of scrolling through endless online stores that don't ship to your area? Welcome to VigyBag - the ultimate online shopping destination for those looking to shop local! With our location-based platform, you can easily find and buy products from your favorite nearby shops.

## ‚úî Features <a name="features"></a>
- Search for products from various broad categories.
- Get the best products at competitive prices.
- Local products delivered to your doorstep, just a click away.
- Compare prices and make purchases directly through the website.
